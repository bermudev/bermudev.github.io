I am excited to share a bit about myself with you!

My name is **Carlos**, I graduated in **Industrial Engineering** in **Seville** and I am a **Software Engineer**. I have always been passionate about technology and software development, and thanks to that, I have been working on it remotely over the last years.

## Tools & Technologies

I have a few favorite things when it comes to technology:

* 🐍 **Python**: This is the language I feel most comfortable with and the one I mainly use in my work and in my pet projects. Besides building APIs with frameworks like **Django**, **FastAPI**, and **Flask**, I enjoy exploring Python for automation, backend services, scripting, and experimenting with new libraries and tools in the ecosystem.
* 🐧 **Linux**: Linux is the operating system I use most both professionally and personally. I enjoy experimenting with different distributions and desktop environments, especially across ecosystems like **Arch-based**, **Ubuntu-based**, and **Fedora-based** systems. In my current role I also work with **Windows**, so I regularly switch between environments depending on the project. Fun fact: I still keep an **Ubuntu 7 installation CD** from the days when Canonical used to ship them for free.
* 🤖 **AI & Machine Learning**: I have a strong interest in AI and machine learning and have been exploring various applications of these technologies in areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive modeling — even before the recent AI boom!
* 🖥️ **System administration**: For many years I have managed cloud-based systems and infrastructure. At home I run a small homelab based on Proxmox, where I manage multiple virtual machines and containers to host self-hosted services, experiment with infrastructure, and learn new technologies.
* 🏴‍☠️ **Cybersecurity**: It has always been one of my main hobbies. For more than 10 years I have been analyzing malware, studying attack vectors, ways to protect against remote attacks, and promoting good security practices.

I have also explored web development, with basic knowledge of **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript/TypeScript**, and some experience building SPAs with **Angular**. Recently I’ve been having fun building things with **Astro 🚀** (like this blog!).
