I am excited to share a bit about myself with you!

My name is **Carlos** and I am a **software engineer**. I graduated in Industrial Engineering in Seville and I have always been passionate about technology and software development. Thanks to that, I have been working on it remotely over the last few years.

## Tools & Technologies

I have a few favorite things when it comes to technology:

* 🐍 **Python**: This is the language I feel most comfortable with and the one I mainly use in my work and in my daily life. I am familiar with frameworks like **Django** for API development and libraries like **Pandas** and **NumPy**.
* 🐧 **Linux**: I've always loved trying out new distributions and desktop environments. Linux is the operating system I use in my work and personal life.
* 🤖 **AI & Machine Learning**: I have a strong interest in AI and machine learning and have been exploring various applications of these technologies in areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive modeling.
* 🖥️ **System administration**: For many years I have been able to manage cloud-based systems and currently own a server at home, with several self-hosted services using **Docker** containers and other technologies.
* 🏴‍☠️ **Cybersecurity**: It has always been one of my main hobbies. For more than 10 years I have been analyzing malware, studying attack vectors, ways to protect against remote attacks, and promoting good security practices.

I have also been able to try my hand at web development, with basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript/TypeScript and using frameworks like Angular for the creation of SPAs. And now, Astro 🚀!
